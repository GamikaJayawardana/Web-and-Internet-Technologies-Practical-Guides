Business Class Customers

  • Spacious seating that transforms into a fully flat bed, with privacy dividers and direct aisle access for most seats.
  • Multi-course gourmet meals served on fine china, with an extensive selection of beverages including international wines and cocktails.
  • Priority boarding and baggage handling services to save time and avoid queues.
  • Access to business class lounges equipped with Wi-Fi, workstations, showers, and complimentary snacks and beverages.
  • Complimentary amenity kits with essential travel items including eye masks, socks, lip balm, and lotions.
  • Wide touchscreen entertainment systems with access to the latest movies, games, TV shows, and news channels.
  • Power outlets and USB ports at every seat to keep devices fully charged during the flight.

Premium Economy Customers

  • Extra legroom and wider seats compared to economy class, with enhanced recline for a more comfortable experience.
  • Upgraded meal service with improved dining options and a welcome drink upon boarding.
  • Separate check-in counters and priority boarding ahead of economy class passengers.
  • Increased baggage allowance for both carry-on and checked baggage, offering greater travel flexibility.
  • Personal seatback screens with a wide selection of entertainment options including music, movies, and games.
  • Adjustable headrests, footrests, and additional cushioning for added comfort on long-haul journeys.
  • Complimentary amenity kits with travel essentials like toothbrush, earplugs, eye masks, and socks.

Economy Class Customers

  • Standard reclining seats with adjustable headrests and tray tables for meals, work, or entertainment.
  • Complimentary meals and snacks provided during the flight, with special dietary options available upon request.
  • In-flight entertainment on personal screens offering movies, music, and TV shows.
  • Access to onboard shopping with a selection of items like perfumes, snacks, and souvenirs.
  • Comfortable pricing for budget-conscious travelers while still providing essential services and safety features.
  • Dedicated cabin crew throughout the journey to ensure a safe and comfortable experience.
